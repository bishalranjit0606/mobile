How to Choose Your Perfect Phone

Confused by specs? Here is a quick guide to help you decide.

Camera Quality

Look for "OIS" (Optical Image Stabilization) if you take photos at night. Essential for steady videos. Remember, high megapixels (MP) aren't everything!

Battery Life

5000mAh is the current standard for heavy users. This should easily last you a full day of mixed usage including gaming and streaming.

Processor Speed

Gamers should look for Snapdragon 8 Gen series or Apple Bionic chips. For basic social media, A-series or Snap 600/700 series are sufficient.

Storage Space

We recommend at least 128GB for long-term use. Apps and photos accumulate quickly, and system files take up significant space.
