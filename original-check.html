Verify Your Device Authenticity

Don't get scammed with refurbished or fake phones. Follow these steps to ensure you are buying a genuine product.

1

Check IMEI Number

Dial *#06# on the keypad. A unique 15-digit number will appear. Match this number EXACTLY with the number printed on the back of the box and the bill.

2

Official Warranty Check

Go to the official brand website (e.g., Apple Coverage Check, Samsung Warranty Info) and enter your device's Serial Number or IMEI. It should show the purchase date and warranty status.

3

Physical Inspection

Look for seamless seals and correct logo placement. Fake phones often have slightly blurry text on the back or misaligned camera lenses. Check the screen brightness and touch responsiveness.

4

Software Verification

Connect the phone to a computer with official software (iTunes for iPhone, Smart Switch for Samsung). If the software recognizes the device, it is likely genuine.
